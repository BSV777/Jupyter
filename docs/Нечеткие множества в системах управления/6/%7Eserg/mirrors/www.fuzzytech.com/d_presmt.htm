MICROCHIP TECHNOLOGY AND INFORM ANNOUNCE JOINT
AGREEMENT TO DEVELOP FUZZY LOGIC FOR MCUs

Contact: Veronica Batchelor, Napier Partnership, +44-243-513434

Microchip Technology Inc. of Chandler Arizona, and INFORM GmbH of Aachen, Germany, today announced a strategic partnership agreement to develop and market fuzzy logic development software for Microchip's proprietary 8-bit microcontroller product line.

Microchip, a leader in the design of field-programmable embedded control solutions, and INFORM, a master of fuzzy logic technology, will apply their respective expertise to develop complete, low-cost, easy-to-use fuzzy logic application development tools for Microchip microcontrollers. The companies will also combine efforts to market the technology to the design community through worldwide technical presentations, design seminars and application development.

"Microchip's focus in the consumer, automotive and office automation markets has clearly been elevated with the opportunities presented by this partnership," explained Bill Knauss, Microchip's Development Systems product marketing manager. "Fuzzy logic is a very powerful concept that is gaining worldwide acceptance and has become a natural extension of our already strong array of microcontroller development tools. After evaluating other companies, Microchip saw INFORM as the clear technology for this partnership.

INFORM, rapidly emerging as the international frontrunner in fuzzy logic technology, regards the Microchip 8-bit microcontroller, empowered with RISC architecture and highly efficient development tools, as a perfect engine for faster execution of fuzzy algorithms.

Microchip has made the embedded control market a priority, and recognizing the market's huge growth potential, has established itself as a successful industry leader," said Constantin von Altrock, director of INFORM's Fuzzy Technologies Division. "We look forward to combining fuzzy logic with Microchip's microcontroller technology."

Microchip Technology Inc. is the worldwide leader in the design, manufacture and marketing of field-programmable high-performance 8-bit RISC microcontrollers, complementary ASSP products and related speciality memory products for high-volume embedded control applications. Microchip addresses the growing need for embedded control solutions in the consumer, automotive, office automation, communications and industrial control markets with prodúcts that feature both ROM-based technology and the industry's most economical OTP capability. The company is located outside Phoenix in Chandler, Arizona, where it operates a 0.9 micron wafer fabrication line, and has a wholly-owned subsidiary performing assembly and test in Kaohsiung, Taiwan, as well as sales offices in Europe, Asia, Japan and North America. Microchip is traded on the NASDAQ exchange under the symbol MCHP.

INFORM GmbH develops and markets a variety of fuzzy logic application tools, including fuzzyTECH, one of the industry's leading software design tools. The company has been involved in extensive research and development of fuzzy logic technology for 24 years. INFORM's tools offer the design engineer a graphic and object-oriented development environment and automatically generate highly optimized processor-specific assembly code or high-level (C) code. Company headquarters is located in Aachen, Germany, with its U.S. sales and support division located outside Chicago in Evanston, Illinois.
