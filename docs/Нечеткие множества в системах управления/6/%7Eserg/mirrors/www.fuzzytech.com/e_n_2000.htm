Inform Software Corporation Statement of Year 2000 Compliance for all fuzzyTECH Products

All fuzzyTECH products release 5.11a or later, delivered after July 1, 1998 are Year 2000 compliant when used with MS Windows 95 or later, or with MS Windows NT 4.0 or later.

Written Statement of Compliance

All users of fuzzyTECH that have purchased a Software Update Contract that comprises extended warranty that expires after January 1, 2000, may receive a written statement and warranty of Year 2000 compliance.

How you can update to a Year 2000 compliant version of fuzzyTECH

If you have purchased fuzzyTECH 5.1 between January 1, 1998, and July 1, 1998, you may update your software for free by downloading the latest Maintenance Release from this web server. If you have purchased fuzzyTECH before January 1, 1998, or if you have a fuzzyTECH Release before 5.1, you will have to purchase a Direct Update from us or your local distributor.
