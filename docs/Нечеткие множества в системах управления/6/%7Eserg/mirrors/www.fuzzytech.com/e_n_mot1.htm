Inform and Motorola Demonstrate fuzzyTECH Support for the 68HC12 MCU

Inform and Motorola demonstrate fuzzyTECH support for the 68HC12 microcontroller family, the world's first standard microcontroller with a complete fuzzy instruction set, at the 5th IEEE Conference on Fuzzy Logic in New Orleans and the Embedded Systems Conference in San Jose.

Lotfi Zadeh (middle), Founder of Fuzzy Logic, with Philip Drake of Motorola (left) and Rodney Hemmerlein of Inform Software Corporation (right) at the 5th IEEE International Conference on Fuzzy Logic in New Orleans 1996

Demonstration of the fuzzyTECH MCU-HC12 Edition at the Embedded Systems Conference in San Jose in 1996 by Motorola and Inform.
