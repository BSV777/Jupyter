Business Application Papers

Title Abstract
Customer Profiling with MS Access This paper examines the use of fuzzy logic for customer targeting by employing the VisualBasic interface of fuzzyTECH in MS-Access. You may follow the example on your PC with the fuzzyTECH for Business Demo. If you choose to install the Business examples in the Setup routine, all the files necessary to follow this application paper are installed.
Quality Control with VisualBasic Fuzzy logic is used in this application for the multi-dimensional analysis of product quality. The user interface is programmed in VisualBasic and uses the VisualBasic source code module provided with fuzzyTECH to interface fuzzyTECH with the VisualBasic forms. You may follow the example on your PC with the fuzzyTECH for Business Demo. If you choose to install the Business examples in the Setup routine, all the files necessary to follow this application paper are installed.
Supplier Evaluation for Sample Testing This application paper introduces the commercial INFORM software products Invent/W and Invent/R that use a fuzzy logic supplier evaluation module for sample testing of both incoming goods (Invent/W) and incoming invoices (Invent/R). By reducing complete inspections to sample tests, a significant amount of labor can be saved.
Customer Targeting This business application paper introduces examples of what can be done with fuzzy logic in the field of marketing.
Sequencing and Scheduling The focus of this application paper is on the merit judgment of a possible solution as one method required for all sequence and schedule optimization processes.
Optimizing Research and Development Projects This paper covers the application of fuzzy logic in a development maturity evaluation system at Mercedes Benz Corporation of Germany. The objective of the project was to optimize the design process of truck components, such as gear boxes, axles, or steering.
Knowledge-Based Prognosis Prognosis is the "black art" of predicting the future. It is used in many different areas and works basically on a defined system. Often, the system can at best be only partially described by a mathematical model. In these cases, human expertise about the system behavior or simply the experience of observing the system's behavior over time must also be considered.
Other Business Applications This paper contains brief abstracts of other business applications using fuzzy logic.

Financial Application Papers

Title Abstract
Stock Prognosis with MS Excel In this paper, we show how fuzzy logic is used to automate stock trade decisions. The fuzzy logic functions were embedded using the fuzzyTECH Function Assistant in MS Excel that is provided with fuzzyTECH. You may follow the example on your PC with the fuzzyTECH for Business Demo. If you choose to install the Business examples in the Setup routine, all the files necessary to follow this application paper are installed.
Fuzzy Scoring for Mortgage Applicants This case study presents an application that INFORM did for a Home & Savings bank in the area of mortgage application assessment.
Creditworthiness Assessment This paper presents the ASK decision-support system based on fuzzy logic. This application was first installed in a Swiss bank in 1986, and this probably makes it the first commercially available fuzzy logic decision-support system in the world.
Fuzzy-Enhanced Score Card for Leasing Risk Assessment BMW Leasing Bank of Germany and INFORM have developed a fuzzy-enhanced score card system to automate the risk assessment evaluation for car leasing contracts.
Fraud Detection This application uses a fuzzy logic system to evaluate insurance claims in the field of home insurance to assess the likelihood of fraudulent behavior.
Other Finance Applications This paper contains brief abstracts on other financial applications using fuzzy logic.

More Business and Finance Application Papers...

...are presented in the book "Fuzzy Logic and NeuroFuzzy Applications in Business and Finance" (for more information refer to the Literature section). The applications include:

  • Business: supplier evaluation for sample testing, customer targeting, sequencing, scheduling, optimizing R&D projects, knowledge-based prognosis, fuzzy data analysis, hospital stay prediction, TV commercial slot evaluation, address matching, fuzzy cluster analysis, sales prognosis for mail order house, multi-criteria optimization, ...
  • Finance: fuzzy scoring for mortgage applicants, creditworthiness assessment, fuzzy-enhanced score card for lease risk assessment, risk profile analysis, insurance fraud detection, cash supply optimization, foreign exchange trading, insider trading surveillance, investor classification, ...

Many of the applications discussed in this book are also contained as animated software simulations. The book also contains a simulation-only version of fuzzyTECH for Business.
